# Astrocytes
<ins>Astrocytes</ins>: star-shaped non-neuron cells located in the central nervous system that are primarily responsible for protection and maintenance of neuron cells 

<ins>Function in Endothelial Cells</ins>: Regulates and maintains the endothelial cells making up the blood brain barrier

# Pericytes
<ins>Pericytes</ins>: cells surrounding the endothelial cells in the BBB.

<ins>Function in Endothelial Cells</ins>: Polarization (asymmetric difference of charge) of astrocytes, regulation of gene-expression in endothelial cells
